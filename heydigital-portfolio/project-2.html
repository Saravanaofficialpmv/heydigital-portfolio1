Awenests — Shopify E-Commerce for Festive & Lifestyle Products

Read more
  • Client:  Awenests
  • Industry:  E-Commerce (Festive Décor, Gifts, Lifestyle Products)
  • Platform:  Shopify
  • Website:  awenests.com
Project Objective

To design and develop a premium Shopify e-commerce store for Awenests, a lifestyle brand that curates festive décor, gift combos, and seasonal collections. The goal was to create a modern, elegant, and conversion-focused store that reflects the brand’s creativity, trust, and quality.

Our Approach

We focused on blending aesthetic design with seamless functionality to make the shopping experience delightful across all devices.

Shopify Store Setup & Custom Theme Design
Created a fully customized Shopify theme that matched Awenests’ brand tone — elegant, festive, and welcoming. Optimized layouts for product storytelling and visual engagement.

Collection & Product Structuring
Structured products by occasion (Christmas, Halloween, Everyday) and category (Décor, Gifts, Combos) for smooth navigation. Added dynamic product filters and “Featured Collections” for easy discovery.

Brand Identity & Visual Consistency
Designed banners, typography, and iconography that convey premium yet festive appeal. Implemented a warm, neutral color palette for a high-end shopping aesthetic.

User Experience & Conversion Optimization
Simplified the cart and checkout flow. Integrated trust elements — “Premium Quality,” “Honest Prices,” “Fast Delivery,” and “Curated Gifts.” Ensured global compatibility for customers shopping from the U.S. and beyond.

SEO & Analytics Integration
Added SEO metadata, structured data, and integrated Google Analytics & Meta Pixel for campaign tracking.

Key Features Delivered
  • Custom Shopify Theme built from scratch
  • Product & Collection Management tailored for seasonal campaigns
  • Mobile-First Design optimized for conversions
  • Integrated Payment Gateways for global customers
  • Festive Marketing Banners & Popups for special offers
  • SEO-Friendly URL Structure and analytics tracking
  • Quick Contact & WhatsApp Chat Integration
Results
  • 60% improvement in homepage engagement after redesign
  • Increased average session time and product views
  • Streamlined shopping flow — leading to higher checkout completions
  • Stronger brand identity that resonates with festive buyers
Client Feedback

“HeyDigitals delivered exactly what we envisioned — a beautiful Shopify store that truly reflects our brand. The user experience is smooth, and our customers love the design.”

Project Outcome

Awenests’ Shopify store now serves as a visually cohesive, high-performing e-commerce platform — built for seasonal launches, international customers, and long-term growth.
